Text Scanner – Image to Text

Upload a screenshot or photo and extract selectable text directly in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

1. Upload image

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP. For best results use clear, high‑contrast images.

Or drag & drop an image file onto this box.
Processing happens locally using Tesseract.js OCR in your browser.

2. Extracted text

The recognized text appears below. You can edit or copy it.

Accuracy depends on image quality, language, and fonts. Try to avoid blurry or very small text.
